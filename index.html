Privacy Policy — Smart Healthcare

Effective Date: November 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Healthcare collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Introduction

Smart Healthcare ("we", "our", or "the App") is designed to help users connect with doctors, view hospital details, book appointments, and get AI-powered guidance. We value your privacy and commit to processing your data transparently and securely.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following kinds of data:

  • Account Information: name, email, phone number, gender, age, profile details.
  • Login Data: securely stored credentials or Google login data.
  • Health Inputs: symptoms, queries typed into the AI medical chat.
  • Usage Activity: screens visited, app features used, appointment actions.
  • Device Data: model, OS version, app version, crash logs.
  • Location Information: only when permitted to show nearby hospitals.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Operate and improve the Smart Healthcare app
  • Enable appointment booking with doctors
  • Provide AI-based medical assistance
  • Send important health reminders or notifications
  • Fix bugs and enhance user experience
  • Protect users from fraud or misuse

4. AI Medical Chat Disclaimer

The AI offers general medical guidance based on user input. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We never sell your personal data.
  • Service Providers: analytics, hosting, authentication services.
  • Legal Requirements: shared only when legally required.
  • With Your Consent: if you request or initiate sharing.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may include third-party tools (analytics, crash reporting, authentication providers). These services handle data under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and strong access controls. While we take strict precautions, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is wiped within 48 hours.

9. Child Privacy

Smart Healthcare is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Your Rights

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Update or correct your profile
  • Delete your account & all associated data
  • Disable permissions such as location access

11. Delete Account

You may delete your account anytime using the link below:

https://smart_healthcare.com/delete-account

All your personal data will be permanently removed within 48 hours.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Using the app after updates implies acceptance of the new terms.
